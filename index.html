About Me

I’m Manav, a developer passionate about startups and innovation. Currently, I’m 3rd year of B-Tech IT, where I manage Frontend development for projects. Additionally, I am the founder of SPARSH by R Naturals is an Indian online store—based in Gujarat—that offers premium, handmade natural hair-care products. Their portfolio includes shampoos, masks, oils, gels, and natural dyes, all crafted in small batches using organic and herbal ingredients with no harsh chemicals. The aim: to nourish the scalp, promote healthy hair growth, and restore shine—all through traditional, time‑tested methods. Since I started my journey in the digital world in 2023, I first immersed myself in design before moving into development. I followed a varied learning path, acquiring different skills that proved valuable and allowed me to develop strong lateral thinking abilities. Here, I covered both design and development roles simultaneously. I am open to new collaboration opportunities and available to discuss interesting projects. Moreover, I have been passionate about launching side projects since the beginning. SPARSH – E-commerce Website
